## Key takeaways for item-type modeling
1. **Official PSSA ELA item taxonomy (per DFA) = two buckets:** "Selected-Response & Technology-Enhanced Questions" (collapsed together) + "Constructed-Response Questions." The DFA does NOT enumerate the TE subtypes (drop-down, hot-text, drag-drop, EBSR, etc.) — those are sub-formats under the "Technology-Enhanced" umbrella.
2. **Constructed-Response** appears once per grade in Section 2 (and additionally Gr8 Sec3) — consistent with one TDA / extended-response per form.
3. The **DRC INSIGHT toolset** (highlighter, sticky note, zoom, line-reader, answer-eliminator, flag) defines the student tooling our renderer should mirror.
4. The i-Ready screenshots are **mechanics reference only** — they map cleanly onto our controlled vocabulary (Drop-down, Drag-and-Drop, Matching, MC) and are good UX models, but none are PSSA-authentic items.
