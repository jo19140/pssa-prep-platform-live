## Renaissance STAR (29 screenshots)

Every item observed is single-select Multiple Choice (3 or 4 options); the only structural variation is cloze-sentence vocabulary vs. short-passage comprehension.

## i-Ready (15 screenshots)

All from a single Level E / Level 2 lesson, "Comparing Text Structures: Part 2" (Instruction and Quiz phases). Level E ≈ Grade 5. Passages: "Rescuing Orphan Elephants" (Amy Tao) and a Stubby/Carl Spitz war-dog pair. The UI is a two-pane "read on left, interact on right" tutor with audio narration (speaker icons), an animated coach avatar, "Find Your Clue" highlighting prompts, and a "Slow Down — please read carefully" pacing nudge. Item formats are richer than STAR.
