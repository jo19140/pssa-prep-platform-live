## After sending — what to expect

**Within 1-2 business days:** Initial response with a quote (typically $500-1,500 for clearance + filing flat fee, plus $250-350 per class in USPTO filing fees).

**1-2 weeks after engagement:** Written clearance opinion delivered. If "GO," they'll prepare the application.

**2-4 weeks after engagement:** Application filed with USPTO. You receive a serial number and confirmation. **Your priority date is now established** — even if registration takes another year, you have priority over anyone who files after this date.

**6-9 months after filing:** USPTO examiner reviews. May approve or issue an office action (questions/objections to resolve). Attorney handles responses.

**3 months after approval:** Publication for opposition period (30 days). If unopposed, registration issues.

**Total timeline:** Typically 8-12 months from engagement to registered mark. You can use "TM" symbol from day one; you can use "®" only after registration issues.
