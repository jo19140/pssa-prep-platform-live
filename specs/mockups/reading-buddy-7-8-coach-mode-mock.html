The rule

Silent e changes the vowel sound.

instructionno score

Silent e changes the vowel to its long sound.

When a short word adds silent e, the vowel changes: cap → cape. The e is silent.

Story

listen & encourageno word-level judgment
Coach lineYou can listen first or read on your own. I won’t score the story yet — I’ll listen and keep you moving.

Jake Stays In

Jake came late to the game. He sat on the bench and felt bad. The club had a rough game, and Max had to run fast. At last, Jake got in. Max gave him a pass, but Jake missed it. The ball hit the net. Jake did not quit. He took his place and kept his eyes on the ball. The ball came back fast. Jake ran to the line and made the save. Max gave Jake a clap. Jake was not the best. He was brave, and he kept at it.

NOTE (Claude): placeholder content — “rough”, “Stays/stayed”, “eyes” are NOT a_e-entry decodable. Final story must pass the content-v3 decodability gates.
