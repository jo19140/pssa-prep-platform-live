### pssa_mock_ebsr_01

**EBSR / two_point**

Part A asks why the walkers slow down. Part B asks for two details that support the Part A answer.

Part A: Choose one answer. Part B: Choose two details.

> The class walked after sunset with paper lanterns. When the path turned muddy, Ms. Cole raised her hand. The group slowed down, stepped on the flat stones, and kept the lanterns high so everyone could see.

Part A
A. The path becomes harder to walk on safely.
B. The lanterns are too heavy to carry.
C. The class wants to end the walk early.
D. Ms. Cole forgets the way back.

Part B
1. the path turned muddy
2. stepped on the flat stones
3. walked after sunset with paper lanterns
4. kept the lanterns high
